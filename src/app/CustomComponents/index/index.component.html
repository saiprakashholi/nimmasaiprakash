Located in the Chikkamagaluru taluk, Chikkamagaluru District of Karnataka, Baba Budangiri/ Datta Peeta is known for its shrine to the Guru Dattatreya and Hazrat Dada Hayath Mir Khalandar, a pilgrimage site for both Hindus and Muslims
